— About us —

About Our Eco Farm

We believe in sustainability and care for environment.

Fresh Station Eco Farm is a lifetime project of Jane and Will Barso, who started it as an exemplary way of living in harmony with the environment and eating healthy.

Today, we are happy to cherish the full-functioning farm that was once a 20-acres amateur project, providing the customers with fresh products and teaching the ways to be more friendly to our planet.
